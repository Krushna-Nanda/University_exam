# **MODULE – I (ENTREPRENEURSHIP BASICS)**

---

## **1. Concept of Entrepreneurship**

**Easy meaning:**
Entrepreneurship = identifying an opportunity + taking risk + organizing resources to create a business.

**Example:**
Opening a cloud kitchen after noticing increasing online food orders → entrepreneurship.

### **2-mark answer format:**

Entrepreneurship is the process of identifying business opportunities, arranging resources, taking risks, and creating value through a new venture.

---

## **2. Intrapreneurship**

**Meaning:**
Intrapreneur = Entrepreneur inside a company.

**Example:**
A Google employee creating Gmail internally = intrapreneurship.

---

## **3. Types of Entrepreneurs** (Easy list: I-TSSS)

1. **Innovative** – create new products (e.g., Elon Musk).
2. **Traditional** – follow existing business models.
3. **Serial** – start multiple ventures.
4. **Social** – solve social problems (e.g., NGOs).
5. **Solo/First-generation** – no family background.

# **MODULE – III (ACCOUNTING, MARKETING, LABOUR LAWS)**

---

## **1. Basics of Accounting Terms (SUPER IMPORTANT)**

### **Assets:**

Things owned by business (cash, building, machines).

### **Liabilities:**

Obligations (loans, creditors).

### **Equity/Capital:**

Owner’s money invested in business.

### **Revenue:**

Income earned from sales.

### **Expenses:**

Costs incurred (salary, rent).

### **Working Capital:**

Current Assets – Current Liabilities.
Indicates daily operating liquidity.

---

# **MODULE – IV (SICK INDUSTRIES & REVIVAL)**

---

## **1. Meaning of Sickness**

A sick industry = a business that cannot meet expenses, repay loans, or run operations.

---

## **2. Symptoms of Sickness**

Easy: FPLD
F – Falling sales
P – Poor cash flow
L – Low capacity use
D – Delay in payments

---

## **3. Causes of Sickness**

**Internal Causes**

* Poor management
* Lack of planning
* Wrong pricing
* Inefficient workers

**External Causes**

* Market recession
* Shortage of raw materials
* Government restriction
* Competition

---

## **4. Cures of Sickness (Revival Measures)**

* Change management
* Restructure loans
* Improve marketing
* Reduce cost
* Infuse new capital

---

## **5. Role of Banks**

* Provide working capital
* Restructure loan
* Provide expert guidance
* Give rehabilitation packages

---

## **6. Role of Government**

* Subsidies
