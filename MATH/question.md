1. Prove that:
   1 + 2 + 3 + ... + n = n(n + 1) / 2
   for all natural numbers n.

2. Prove that:
   2 + 4 + 6 + ... + 2n = n(n + 1)
   for all natural numbers n.

3. Prove that:
   1^2 + 2^2 + 3^2 + ... + n^2 = n(n + 1)(2n + 1) / 6
   for all natural numbers n.

4. Prove that:
   3 + 6 + 9 + ... + 3n = 3n(n + 1) / 2
   for all natural numbers n.

5. Prove that:
   1 + 3 + 5 + ... + (2n - 1) = n^2
   for all natural numbers n.
